About Us

Who we are

Since becoming an independent company in 2009, BAT has expanded on its position in growing in the end market we serve.

With the engineering global demand for power and energy, BAT use of the latest technology and ability to deliver value as one of the fastest growing members of the power generating market. We have set our sights on contributing to a sustainable future that aligns with our good environmental stewardship.

Company History

BAT TECH & ENGINEERING WORKS has been and remained pioneers in the field of diesel and petrol engines with repairs and maintenance since its creation in 2009.

In addition to developing many innovative engineering solutions for the auto industry, BAT ENGINEERING has also contributed to technology transfer and sustainable development sharing innovative ideas and project execution methods.

Our Values Solidify Who and What We Believe

BAT TECH & ENGINEERING WORKS Values are:

  • Uncompromising commitment to quality health, safety and environment.
  • An open relationship with our employees based on mutual trust, respect and success.
  • Transparency, Accountability and Discipline in our work.
  • Best in class risk Awareness.
  • Integrity in all we do.
  • It is vital to keep your operations up and running your people safe and do it all at a profit.

Mission, Vision, Values

BAT TECH & ENGINEERING WORKS core values drive everything we do and incorporating these core values in our daily work and making them integral part of our culture is key to our future success. Our vision reminds each of us of what we should be striving for each day and when as a company.
